# Marketplace Product Seller Agreement

Version: v1

This agreement governs creator listings submitted to the Melogic Records marketplace. By accepting this agreement, you confirm that you have the rights required to upload, sell, license, and distribute the product files, previews, artwork, metadata, and related materials you provide.

## Seller Responsibilities

- You are responsible for the accuracy of your product title, descriptions, pricing, media, deliverables, and contributor information.
- You must not upload material that infringes another party's rights or violates applicable law.
- You must provide usable product files and previews that match the listing description.
- You must resolve contributor permissions, royalties, and credits before submitting a product for marketplace review.

## Marketplace Review

Melogic Records may review, reject, remove, hide, or request changes to marketplace listings for quality, rights, policy, safety, or platform integrity reasons.

## Payments And Fees

Marketplace pricing, platform fees, processor fees, payout timing, and related payment terms may be configured by Melogic Records and may change over time. The product editor displays current pricing calculations where available.

## Updates

Melogic Records may update this seller agreement. If a newer version is active, sellers may be required to accept the latest version before publishing or submitting listings for review.

## Acceptance

By signing electronically in the product editor, you agree to this Marketplace Product Seller Agreement for the listing workflow and confirm that the information you provide is accurate to the best of your knowledge.
